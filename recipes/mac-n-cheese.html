Mac N' Cheese

Description

Classic old school mac n' cheese with big portions.

Ingredients

  • 7/4 pounds whole-wheat macaroni
  • 3/4 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 6 cups milk, divided
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon mustard powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 8 ounce shredded cheddar cheese
  • 24 ounce shredded American cheese
  • 8 ounce bag of potato chips, crushed
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/3 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • butter-flavored cookiing spray

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Boil a large pot of lightly salted water. Cook macaroni in boiling water, stirring occasionally for about 8 minutes.
  3. Melt butter in a large pot over medium-low heat, slowly add flour and whisk constantly. Cook until brown and the mixture no longer smells of flour, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour 1 cup milk into the flour mixture, whisking continually until fully incorporated, about 45 seconds; repeat twice.
  5. Add remaining 3 cups milk to the mixture, whisking to incorporate.
  6. Stir Worcestershire sauce, mustard powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper into the mixture; season with salt and black pepper.
  7. Reduce heat to low and continue to cook sauce, whisking frequently until it begins to thicken, about 10 minutes.
  8. Add about 4 ounces of shredded cheddar cheese; stir continually until the cheese melts completely. Repeat with remaining cheddar cheese and American cheese, about 4 ounces at a time. Once cheese is entirely incorporated, remove sauce from heat.
  9. Stir drained macaroni into the cheese sauce to coat.
  10. Divide macaroni between two 9x13-inch baking dishes.
  11. Mix crushed potato chips, 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, and parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  12. Top the macaroni with the potato chip mixture evenly.
  13. Spray the potato chip mixture with cooking spray.
  14. Bake in preheated oven until the crust is golden brown and the sauce is bubbling, about 35 to 45 minutes.
