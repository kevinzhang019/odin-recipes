Chicken Wings

Description

Learn to cook delicious and crispy baked chicken wings, requiring minimal preparation. All you need is oil, seasoning, an oven or airfyer, and of course, chicken wings.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves pressed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt
  • ground black pepper
  • 10 chicken wings

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Add olive oil, garlic, chili powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper to a resealable bag and shake.
  3. Add chicken wings to the resealable bag and shake.
  4. Arrange chicken wings on a baking sheet and prevent them from touching.
  5. Cook chicken wings in the oven after preheating for about 1 hour.
  6. Serve and enjoy your delicious and crispy wings.
