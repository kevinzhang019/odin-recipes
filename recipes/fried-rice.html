Fried Rice

Description

A quick and easy fried rice recipe. Learn to make fried rice like your favorite chinese restaurant.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked white rice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil

Steps

  1. Place carrots in a saucepan and cover with water. Bring to a low boil and cook for 3-5 minutes, then stir in peas and immediately drain in a colander.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a wok over high heat.
  3. Stir in carrots, peas, and garlic and cook for 30 seconds.
  4. Add eggs and stir quickly to scramble with vegetables.
  5. Stir in cooked rice, add soy sauce, and toss rice. Add sesame rice and toss rice again.
  6. Stir and enjoy your delicious fried rice.
